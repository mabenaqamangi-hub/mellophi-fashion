Returns & Exchange Policy

At Mellophi Fashion, we value your support and want you to love every purchase.

Important: We do not offer returns or refunds. Exchanges are available within 3 days under the conditions outlined below.

Exchange Policy

Eligibility for Exchange

To be eligible for an exchange, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Exchange request made within 3 days of delivery or collection
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and unused
  • Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached
  • Items must be in the original packaging
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) must be provided

What Can Be Exchanged

  • Size exchanges: Different size of the same item
  • Color exchanges: Different color of the same item (subject to availability)
  • Defective items: Items with manufacturing defects or damage during shipping

What Cannot Be Exchanged

  • Items worn, washed, or altered in any way
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Items purchased during clearance or final sale (if applicable)
  • Items requested for exchange after the 3-day window

How to Request an Exchange

  1. Contact Us Within 3 Days:

    Email us at info@mellophifashion.co.za or WhatsApp +27 650 458 081 with:

    • Your order number
    • Item(s) you wish to exchange
    • Reason for exchange (size/color/defect)
    • Photos of the item (if defective)
  2. Await Confirmation:

    We will confirm if the requested size/color is available and provide return instructions.

  3. Return the Item:

    Package the item securely in its original packaging and send it back to us. You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we sent the wrong item.

  4. Inspection & Processing:

    Once we receive your return, we'll inspect it within 2 business days. If approved, we'll ship your exchange item immediately.

  5. Receive Your Exchange:

    Your new item will be shipped to you at no additional cost (standard shipping).

Shipping & Costs

Return Shipping

  • Customer responsibility: You pay for return shipping unless the item is defective or incorrect
  • Our responsibility: We cover return shipping for defective or incorrect items

Exchange Shipping

  • We cover standard shipping for your exchange item
  • Express shipping available at an additional cost

Lost or Damaged Returns

We recommend using a trackable shipping method. Mellophi Fashion is not responsible for items lost or damaged during return transit.

No Returns & No Refunds

Please note: We do not accept returns for refunds. All sales are final.

Why No Refunds?

As a small business offering affordable luxury, this policy allows us to:

  • Keep our prices competitive
  • Maintain quality control of our inventory
  • Ensure all items sold are in pristine condition

Exceptions

Refunds will only be considered in the following circumstances:

  • You received a completely different item than ordered
  • The item arrived with a significant defect that cannot be exchanged
  • We are unable to provide an exchange due to stock unavailability

In such cases, please contact us at info@mellophifashion.co.za and we will review your request on a case-by-case basis.
