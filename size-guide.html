How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape horizontal.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape comfortably loose.
  • Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 20cm below your waist.
  • Length: Measure from the highest point of your shoulder down to your desired hem length.
  • Note: If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.
  • Contact Us: Still unsure? Our team is here to help! Contact us for personalized sizing advice.
