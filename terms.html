Terms & Conditions

Returns & Exchange Policy

At Mellophi Fashion, we value your support and want you to enjoy every purchase. Please note our policy: no returns and no refunds. Exchanges are allowed under the conditions below.

Eligibility for Exchange

  • Exchanges must be requested within 3 days of delivery or collection.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, unused, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

Exchange Process

  1. Contact us within 3 days of receiving your order, including your order details and the item you'd like to exchange.
  2. We will confirm the availability of the new size or colour.
  3. Once the original item is returned and inspected, your exchange will be processed.

Important Notes

  • No refunds after purchase.
  • Customers are responsible for return courier costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Shipping & Delivery Policy

Mellophi Fashion ensures that your orders are packaged and delivered with care. Delivery times and charges depend on your location and courier service.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days after payment is received. You'll receive a confirmation once your order has been shipped.

Delivery Time

  • Local deliveries: 2–5 business days
  • Regional deliveries: 3–7 business days

Shipping Details

  • Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery information.
  • Mellophi Fashion is not liable for delays caused by couriers or incorrect addresses.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as your name, email, and delivery address.
  • Payment details processed securely through trusted gateways.
  • Website usage information via cookies to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver orders.
  • To communicate order updates and promotions.
  • To enhance website functionality and user experience.

Data Protection

We use secure servers and encryption to safeguard your information. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except as required by law.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us via our Contact Page.
